Life insurance is a contract between an insurance company and a policyholder , in which the insurer guarantees to pay a designated sum of money to the policyholder 's beneficiaries upon the policyholder's death, in exchange for the payment of premiums. There are two main types of life insurance: term life and permanent life. Term life insurance provides coverage for a specific period of time, while permanent life insurance provides coverage for the policyholder's entire life. The best life insurance companies offer a range of policy types, affordable premiums, and high customer satisfaction. When shopping for life insurance, it's important to consider your financial situation, the needs of your beneficiaries, and the reputation of the insurance company.
